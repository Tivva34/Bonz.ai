Our story

Bonz.ai was created in 2023 by Jesper Nyberg from Sweden.

After enduring a dreadful experience at a poorly managed hotel, Jesper decided to change the world.

Fueled by determination, he programmed the most realistic AI team ever created. When he unveiled his groundbreaking work on X, it quickly caught the attention of none other than Elon Musk.

Impressed and inspired, Elon offered to invest in Jesper's ambitious vision of an AI-driven hotel empire, providing a staggering 13 quadrillion dollars to bring the dream to life.

Jesper wasted no time, purchasing land in every country and even founding two entirely new nations. With a portion of the funds, he commissioned the construction of the world's largest 3D printer, capable of producing a revolutionary concept he called AI-hotels.

As the hotels took shape, Jesper deployed his elite AI team, the Jesperonius-4, to oversee operations, revolutionizing the hotel industry and leaving no corner untouched.

The impact was undeniable. Entire industries trembled as his empire expanded. Hotels.com famously declared, "Jesper ruined us," a quote now etched in history as a testament to one of the most transformative—and controversial—ventures of all time.
